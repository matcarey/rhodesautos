Because all cars need Rhodes

We are a family run car repair company. We provide a wealth of garage services to both domestic and commercial customers. Here at Rhodes Autos we know that there is no such thing as a simple car repair when it comes to your vehicle, because whether you use it for work or home, your car is essential to you.

Services and MOT testing

We strive to provide each of our customers with the peace of mind that their vehicle is in excellent hands. Our Head technician Michael is a highly experienced mechanic, he is NVQ and City and Guilds trained in a wide variety of differing and unique mechanical expertise, to make sure that you receive a service that is second to none. We can M.O.T test your vehicle, carry out repairs and provide full annual or interim services. We use the latest diagnostics equipment on your vehicle to ensure any issues are identified and rectified as efficiently as possible. Among the services that we are able to provide you are;

  • Engine repairs
  • Gearbox maintenance
  • Light vehicle maintenance 
  • Diagnostics
  • MOT testing and repairs
  • Air conditioning re-gassing and repairs
  • Brakes and clutches
  • Exhausts
  • Batteries
  • Suspension
  • Timing Belts
  • Vehicle Servicing

We also offer a collections and drop off service within the local area, and beyond!
